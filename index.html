FESTIVAL 2020

The CAPTCHA DESIGN FESTIVAL will be taking place for the 7th time this August. It is organized by students of the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim.

Facing the current threat of the coronavirus, we have decided to digitalise this year’s festival. True to our tradition, there will be an initial single-day symposium, a following week of four different workshops, and a final virtual exhibition. Workshop leaders and participants worldwide connect and communicate via digital tools.

We are eagerly working on creating a virtual experience close to real life. Be prepared for a futuristic workspace, interdisciplinary projects and an exchange of extraordinary ideas.

As organizers we are aware of our responsibility towards all designers and participants and take the spread of this virus very seriously. So stay safe, stay home and #FlattenTheCurve!

HABITAT

In a biological context, the term HABITAT is used to describe the natural environment for living organisms of all kinds. As we inhabit cities and villages, microorganisms such as bacteria and viruses inhabit us.

In our modern society especially during the ongoing pandemic, we do not simply live in thenatural, real world. Our minds have merged with another non-physical habitat: the digital one. Within our online design festival Captcha 2020 we create spheres in which we explore the limits, variety and endangering of habitats.
