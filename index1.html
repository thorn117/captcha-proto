WE ARE DIGITAL

The CAPTCHA Design Festival will be taking place for the 7th time this August. It is organized by students of the University of Applied Sciences Mannheim.

Meeting this year in Mannheim might be impossible for most of us. Yet, Captcha is an amazing event and we will not cancel it. Therefore we have decided to provide you with a virtual Captcha Version. True to our tradition, there will be an initial single-day symposium, a following week of four different workshops, and a final virtual exhibition. Workshop leaders and participants worldwide connect and communicate via digital tools. We are eagerly working on creating a virtual experience close to real life.

HABITAT

from lat. habitāre | [ˈhæb.ə.tæt]

This year's topic for the festival is HABITAT. In a biological context, the term is used to describe the natural environment for living organisms of all kinds. As we inhabit cities and villages, microorganisms such as bacteria and viruses inhabit us.

In our modern society especially during the ongoing pandemic, we do not simply live in the natural, real world. Our minds have merged with another non-physical habitat: the digital one. Within our online design festival Captcha 2020 we create spheres in which we explore the limits, variety and endangering of habitats.

Be prepared for a futuristic workspace, interdisciplinary projects and an exchange of extraordinary ideas.

As organizers we are aware of our responsibility towards all designers and participants and take this pandemic very seriously. So stay safe, stay home and #FlattenTheCurve!
